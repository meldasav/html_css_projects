Elon Musk

Elon Reeve Musk (born June 28, 1971) is an entrepreneur and business magnate.
He is the founder, CEO and Chief Engineer at SpaceX; early-stage investor, CEO and Product Architect of Tesla, Inc.
Musk is the richest person in the world.

Musk was born to a Canadian mother and South African father and raised in Pretoria, South Africa.
He briefly attended the University of Pretoria before moving to Canada aged 17 to attend Queen's University.
He moved to California in 1995 to attend Stanford University.
Musk co-founded online bank X.com that same year, which merged with Confinity in 2000 to form PayPal.
